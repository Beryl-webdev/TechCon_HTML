Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small gathering of tech enthusiasts and has grown into a global conference attracting thousands of professionals, innovators, and students each year. From its humble beginnings in a local community center to its current international scale, TechCon has always focused on pushing the boundaries of innovation.

Our Mission

TechCon's mission is to inspire, educate, and connect the global tech community. We strive to create a platform where brilliant minds can share ideas, spark innovation, and lead the future of technology. Our commitment to inclusivity, accessibility, and forward-thinking topics sets us apart.

Notable Past Speakers

Dr. Jane Smith

Renowned AI researcher and author of "Future of Intelligence", Dr. Smith spoke at TechCon 2019 on the ethical implications of artificial intelligence.

Elijah Tan

Founder of Code4Good, Elijah inspired audiences in 2021 with his insights on how coding can solve real-world social issues.

Sara Al-Khalifa

A cybersecurity expert and former CTO of CyberTech Global, Sara emphasized the importance of secure digital infrastructures at TechCon 2022.
